If you want to combine consulting with AI executive capabilities, you can add a subscription to your consulting engagement at any time. See Section 7 for details.

## Section 4: The Operating Simply Scorecard

### What the Scorecard Measures

The Operating Simply Scorecard assesses your business across ten pillars, each scored from 1 to 10:

| Pillar | What It Measures |
|--------|------------------|
| **Vision** | Clear direction and future state — where you're going |
| **Mission** | Purpose and core activities — what you do and why |
| **Values** | Guiding principles and beliefs — how you operate |
| **Strategy** | Approach to achieving goals — your path to success |
| **People** | Team building and culture — who you have and how they work together |
| **Process** | Operations and workflows — how work gets done |
| **Product** | Offerings and quality — what you sell and how good it is |
| **Finance** | Financial health and management — the numbers that matter |
| **Marketing** | Brand and customer acquisition — how you attract business |
| **Growth** | Scaling and expansion — your capacity to get bigger |

### How Your Initial Assessment Works

During your Business Analysis or first consulting session:

1. You complete the intake survey sharing your goals, challenges, and context
2. Your consultant reviews your responses and conducts discovery
3. Together, you score each of the ten pillars based on current state
4. Scores are recorded as your baseline assessment

This becomes your "before" snapshot for tracking progress.

### How to Read Your Scorecard Visualization

In HQ Keystones, your scorecard appears as a radar chart:

- **Each axis** represents one of the ten pillars
- **The score (1-10)** determines how far the shape extends on that axis
- **Higher scores** mean the shape extends further from center
- **Lower scores** mean the shape stays closer to center
- **A balanced business** has a relatively even shape
- **Gaps** indicate pillars that need attention

The color of each pillar score indicates health:
- **Green (8-10)** — Strong, healthy
- **Cyan (6-7)** — Good, room for improvement
- **Amber (4-5)** — Needs attention
- **Red (1-3)** — Critical, requires immediate focus

### Tracking Your Progress (Before/After)

As your engagement progresses, your consultant takes scorecard snapshots. The comparison view shows:

- Your original baseline scores
- Your current scores
- Change indicators for each pillar (positive or negative)
- Total improvement across all pillars
- Average score improvement

This visualizes the tangible progress your consulting work produces.

## Section 5: Working With Your Consultant

### What to Expect During Your Engagement

A typical consulting engagement follows this flow:

1. **Intake** — You complete the business analysis survey
2. **Discovery** — Initial call to understand your situation deeply
3. **Assessment** — Scorecard creation and priority identification
4. **Work Sessions** — Focused calls to work through priorities
5. **Deliverables** — Documents, templates, and systems created for you
6. **Check-ins** — Follow-up calls to ensure implementation

Your consultant works on your schedule. Sessions are typically scheduled via Calendly, and you can see your upcoming appointments in your dashboard.

### How Deliverables Are Shared

All deliverables from your engagement are:

1. Uploaded to your HQ Documents section
2. Marked with status (pending, in progress, complete)
3. Available for download at any time

Deliverables may include:
- Strategy documents
- Process maps and SOPs
- Templates and checklists
- Meeting notes and action items
- Recommendations and reports

### How Time Tracking Works

Your consultant logs time for every work session. You can view:

- **Date** — When the work occurred
- **Hours** — Time spent
- **Description** — What was accomplished
- **Category** — Type of work (discovery, documentation, training, etc.)

Time is logged in actual increments, not rounded arbitrarily. You see exactly where your consulting investment goes.

### Communication Expectations

- **Response Time** — Your consultant typically responds within 24 business hours
- **Scheduling** — Book calls through the provided Calendly link
- **Urgent Matters** — Flag urgent items in your message subject line
- **Email** — Direct email to your consultant for questions between calls
- **Preferred Contact** — Indicate your preference in your intake survey

## Section 8: Frequently Asked Questions

### Account and Access

**How do I reset my password?**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page at app.zanderos.com and follow the email instructions.

**Can I have multiple users on my account?**
Consulting accounts support one primary user. If you need team access, ask your consultant about adding team members or upgrading to a subscription tier.

**How long do I have access to HQ after my engagement ends?**
Your HQ access and all uploaded documents remain available for 12 months after your engagement ends. After that, you can maintain access by purchasing an Extension or subscribing to a platform tier.

### Consulting Engagement

**What if I don't use all my hours?**
Unused hours remain available until your engagement timeline expires. If you need more time, purchase an Extension to add three months.

**Can I add more hours to my package?**
Yes. Contact your consultant to purchase additional hours at the ad hoc rate ($250/hour) or upgrade to a larger package.

**What if I need to pause my engagement?**
Life happens. Contact your consultant to discuss pausing your engagement timeline. Extensions are available if needed.

**How do I know if consulting is working?**
Your Operating Simply Scorecard provides objective before/after measurement. You'll see concrete improvement in pillar scores over time.

### Deliverables and Documents

**Where do I find my deliverables?**
All deliverables appear in HQ under the Documents section, marked by status.

**Can I request changes to deliverables?**
Yes. Feedback and revisions are part of your consulting engagement. Discuss needed changes during your sessions.

**Who owns the deliverables?**
You do. All materials created for your business belong to you.

### Upgrading and Platform Access

**Do I need a subscription to use consulting?**
No. Consulting packages work independently of platform subscriptions.

**Can I try the AI executives before subscribing?**
The Free tier provides limited platform access. For a full trial experience, contact your consultant about a demo.

**If I subscribe, does it affect my consulting hours?**
No. Subscription and consulting are separate. Your consulting hours remain unchanged.

### Getting Help

**How do I contact my consultant?**
Email directly or book a call via the Calendly link provided in your welcome email.

**What if I have a technical issue with the platform?**
Email support@zanderos.com with a description of the issue and screenshots if applicable.

**Where can I give feedback about my experience?**
We welcome feedback. Share it directly with your consultant or email feedback@zanderos.com.

# Business Context — Zander Platform

## What is Zander?

Zander is an AI-powered executive assistant platform for small businesses. It provides AI executives that handle specific business functions:

- **Jordan:** CEO functions (strategy, oversight)
- **Don:** CMO functions (marketing, communications)
- **Pam:** EA functions (scheduling, admin)

## Target Market

- Small to medium businesses
- Solo entrepreneurs
- Professional services firms
- Companies without dedicated C-suite

## Value Proposition

1. AI executives available 24/7
2. Consistent, reliable execution
3. Integrated with existing tools (Gmail, Calendar, etc.)
4. Fraction of the cost of human executives

## Revenue Model

- SaaS subscription tiers (see TIERS.md)
- Usage-based pricing for AI tokens
- Enterprise custom pricing

## Competitive Landscape

- General AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude)
- Virtual assistant services
- CRM platforms with AI features
- Marketing automation tools

## Differentiation

1. Role-specific AI executives (not general-purpose)
2. Deep integration with business tools
3. Human-in-the-loop for all external communications
4. Multi-tenant architecture for agencies
